About Us

Taahirah Modelling Agency (TMA) was founded in 2015 by Taahirah Zungu with a vision to create a platform that celebrates African beauty and talent on the global stage.

What started as a small local agency has grown into South Africa's premier modelling management company, representing diverse talent across fashion, commercial, and runway sectors.

Our mission is to empower models through professional development, ethical representation, and opportunities that showcase their unique beauty and personality.
