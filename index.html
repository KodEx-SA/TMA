About Us

Who We Are

Taahirah Modelling Agency (TMA) is South Africa's premier modelling management company, founded in 2015 by Taahirah Zungu. We celebrate African beauty and talent on the global stage.

Our Story

What started as a small local agency has grown into a leading force in South Africa's fashion industry, representing diverse talent across fashion, commercial, and runway sectors.

Achievements

Over 500+ models trained, 50+ fashion shows organized, partnerships with major brands, and recognition as one of North West's leading agencies.

Meet Our Founder

Taahirah Zungu

With over a decade of experience in the fashion industry, Taahirah has dedicated her career to empowering models through professional development, ethical representation, and opportunities that showcase their unique beauty and personality.
